Placement Details

College of Engineering Kallooppara (CEK), located in the Sylvan hills of Pathanamthitta district
in the state of Kerala is one of the premier institutions among the new generation Engineering colleges of South India.
Ever since its inception under the aegis of the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD-Established by the Govt. of Kerala),
the college has already made its presence felt in the technical horizon of the State.
The College is affiliated to the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (APJAKTU) and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE),
